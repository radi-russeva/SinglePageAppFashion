Marianna Mäkelä, 26, founder of Mariannan.com, is a full-time blogger living in the city centre of Helsinki. She is passionate about fashion, beauty, lifestyle, interior design and traveling – as well as writing, photographing and creating new. Relationship with readers is one of the most important things for Marianna, being honest and reliable. Mariannan blog was founded in 2006. Marianna has designed an own cloth collection with her name for BikBok. She has been featured besides Finnish magazines also at Elle and Cosmopolitan international published magazines.
